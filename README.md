# excercise_genome_analysis_northwestern_university

### Excercise Overview

This excercise was carried out to assess participants understanding and ability to analyze genomic datasets after a genomics training anchored by the Centre for Pathogen Genomics and Microbial Evolution, Northwestern University and organized by the Mycology Reference Laboratory, College of Medicine, University of Lagos.
The aim was to analyze long-read sequencing data from *Klebsiella pneumoniae*, pertforming quality control, genome assembly, assembly evaluation and species confirmation.
